Why Kittery Residents Trust Us

Kittery presents a unique combination of plumbing challenges. Many properties here were built in the 18th and 19th centuries, and their plumbing systems have been patched and modified over generations. Aging cast iron drains, lead service lines, and corroded copper are issues we encounter regularly in older Kittery neighborhoods. We have the experience to diagnose these problems accurately and recommend lasting solutions rather than temporary patches.

The town's proximity to the coast and the Piscataqua River also means some homes deal with higher humidity and salt exposure, which can accelerate pipe corrosion and fitting failure. And because Kittery borders Portsmouth, New Hampshire, homeowners here sometimes struggle to find a plumber who's actually licensed in Maine and willing to serve the area promptly. We are -- and we do.
