Why Waterboro Homeowners Call Us

Living in a rural town like Waterboro comes with real plumbing considerations that coastal or suburban homeowners don't face. Well water quality is a major one. High iron content, manganese, hard water minerals, and low pH are common in this area and can wreak havoc on fixtures, appliances, and pipes over time. We help homeowners select and install the right filtration and treatment systems to protect their plumbing investment and improve their water quality.

Waterboro's spread-out geography -- with distinct villages like East Waterboro, North Waterboro, and the Lake Arrowhead community -- means some plumbing companies consider it too far off their route. Not us. We serve all of Waterboro with the same fast response times our customers across Southern Maine expect. Whether you're in a lakeside cabin on Lake Arrowhead or a family home along Route 202, we'll be there.

Maine winters also hit hard out here. Frozen and burst pipes are among the most common emergency calls we take from Waterboro, especially in older homes with inadequate insulation around supply lines. We respond quickly to limit water damage and get your system back online, and we can recommend preventive measures to reduce the risk of it happening again.
