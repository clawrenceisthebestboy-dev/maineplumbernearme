Why OOB Property Owners Choose Us

Old Orchard Beach's plumbing challenges are unique among Southern Maine towns. Many of the homes near the beach were originally built as small summer cottages in the early-to-mid 1900s. Over the years, these structures have been expanded, converted to year-round use, or turned into rental units -- often with plumbing systems that were never upgraded to match. Undersized supply lines, deteriorating drain pipes, and outdated water heaters are issues we see in OOB properties on a weekly basis.

The coastal environment accelerates these problems significantly. Salt air corrosion attacks exposed copper fittings, outdoor hose bibs, and even interior pipes in poorly insulated walls. We regularly help OOB homeowners replace corroded sections and install corrosion-resistant materials that stand up better to the ocean environment.

For rental property owners and managers, we understand the urgency of keeping units functional during tourist season. A plumbing issue that takes days to fix means lost bookings and negative reviews. We offer same-day service and prioritize getting your property back online fast. From seasonal startup inspections in the spring to winterization in the fall, we help OOB property owners protect their investment and avoid costly surprises.
